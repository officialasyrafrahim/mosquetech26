What is Skim Pintar?

Skim Pintar is a community welfare scheme by Masjid Ar-Raudhah. It provides affordable protection for you and your family — including free funeral services and discounts on Islamic courses.

🌙
>$5 and <$20/month
Skim Pintar Basic
  • Free funeral services
  • Ritual bathing and shrouding
  • Burial and transport support
  • 20% discount for courses
⭐
$20 and above/month
Skim Pintar Plus
  • All Skim Pintar benefits included
  • Up to 10 same-address family members
  • Parents and parents-in-law at other address
  • Extended family coverage support
